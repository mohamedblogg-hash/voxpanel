Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

At VoxPanel, accessible from voxpanel.xyz, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by VoxPanel and how we use it.

Information We Collect

VoxPanel follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected by log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks.

These are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable. The purpose of the information is for analyzing trends, administering the site, tracking users' movement on the website, and gathering demographic information.

Privacy Certified & GDPR Compliance

We ensure that all decentralized demographic data streams are handled under strict encryption protocols. We do not sell, rent, or distribute personal identity sequences to unauthorized third-party marketing networks.
